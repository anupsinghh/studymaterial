About This Portal

This portal is designed to help students easily access study materials, including previous year question papers, notes, and other resources. Whether you're preparing for mid-semester exams or final exams, you'll find the resources you need here.

Created by Anup Singh, a passionate backend engineering enthusiast and a third-year computer engineering student at Marwadi University, this platform aims to make studying more efficient and accessible for all students.
